Dopamine Menu is your favorite cozy self-care app designed to help you avoid doomscrolling replacing it with thoughtful, relaxing and intentional activities throught the day facilitating your progress to not only improve your daily habits, but allowing you to track them in a fun lighthearted way and allowing you to be more focused on the present moment reducing your screentime.

Some of the core features include: Custumizable menu, you can fill with the things you want to do. A tracker or receipt that logs the activities done during the week Reward system for each completed actitity.
